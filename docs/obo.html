obo:'acquisition computer' has attributes:
  • Label: 'acquisition computer'
  • Definition: A Computer used for NMR, can be divided into central processing unit (CPU), consisting of instruction, interpretation and arithmetic unit plus fast access memory, and peripheral devices such as bulk data storage and input and output devices (including, via the interface, the spectrometer). Note that devices such as the spectrometer may themselves incorporate small computers.
